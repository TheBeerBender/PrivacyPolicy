Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MelodyScape ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the MelodyScape iOS application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

  • Location Data. With your permission, the App accesses your precise location in the background. Location is used only to detect when you enter or exit the zones you create. Location data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate our own servers; we do not send your location to any service we control. When you use map features (e.g. directions, search, or address lookup), coordinates and queries are sent to Apple’s MapKit services under Apple’s privacy policies. If you enable iCloud Sync (see below), zone data that includes coordinates is stored in your iCloud account.
  • Music Library Data. When you link Apple Music, the App uses MusicKit to browse your library and play songs. We do not copy, upload, or share your music data.
  • User Files. The zones and maps you create are saved on your device. If you turn on iCloud Sync in Settings, those files (including zone locations) are also stored and synced across your devices via your iCloud account, subject to Apple’s privacy policy. If iCloud Sync is off, your files leave the device only when you explicitly export them via the share menu.
  • Photos (optional). If you choose a custom background for the Live Activity (Standby) screen, the App lets you pick an image from your photo library. That image is stored only on your device in the App’s storage and is not shared with us or any third party.
  • Purchase Information. If you choose to purchase a subscription or lifetime upgrade, purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. We do not receive or store your full payment card information. Apple may provide us with limited transaction or entitlement information necessary to confirm your purchase status and unlock premium features. Your purchase is also subject to Apple’s privacy policy and terms.

3. How We Use Information

  • To display your current position on the map.
  • To trigger music playback when you enter or exit a zone.
  • To let you browse and assign tracks from your Apple Music library.
  • To confirm premium purchase status and provide access to premium features.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties (such as advertisers or data brokers). We do not operate our own servers. Location and zone data are not sent to any service we control. If iCloud Sync is enabled, your zones and maps are stored in your iCloud account and synced by Apple. Music playback and map features (directions, search, geocoding) use Apple Music and Apple MapKit under Apple’s privacy policies. Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store.

5. Data Retention

When iCloud Sync is off, all personal data stays on your device until you delete the App or remove it within the App. When iCloud Sync is on, your zones and maps are also stored in your iCloud account; they may remain there after you delete the App until you remove them from iCloud Drive or disable iCloud Sync. You can erase zones at any time from within the App or delete the App to remove data from the device.

6. Your Choices

  • You may revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ Location Services.
  • You may disconnect Apple Music access in iOS Settings ▸ Music ▸ Apps with Access.
  • You may turn off iCloud Sync at any time in the App’s Settings; your data will then be stored only on the device.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data we process about you, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. To exercise these rights, please contact us at melodyscapeapp@gmail.com. Because much of the App’s data is stored on your device or in your personal Apple account, some requests may need to be completed by you directly in the App, in iOS Settings, or through Apple services such as iCloud or the App Store.

8. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page, and the "Effective date" above will be updated. For material changes, we may also provide notice within the App.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at melodyscapeapp@gmail.com.
